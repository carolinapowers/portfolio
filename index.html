Resume

I want to create experiences that satisfy people's needs

In October of 2014, I decided to make a change. I resigned from my job as a Physical Education teacher and invested my time and energy in learning web development at an intensive coding bootcamp. Why? Because I realized that people were passionate about web applications just like I was, and I wanted to be part of the process of making peoples' wishes come true.

Web development is not only about creating and maintaining websites. Web development is about researching the latest technologies, interacting with your fellow developers and designers, thinking about user interactions, and delivering the best solution to your clients. I’m passionate about folowing best practices and creating well written code, therefore I'm always interested in being part of a team that is geared towards the same direction.

Education

Thinkful

After working with AngularJS for nearly one year at Deloitte, I decided to enroll in the Front End AngularJS course offered by Thinkful. The course at Thinkful was written by Matias Niemela, a Google engineer and a core contributor to Angular. The course covered the MVC (Model-View-Controller) model, how to wrap APIs in services, and most importantly (for Angular) how to extend your HTML vocabulary with directives. I made the decision to do this course to gain a thoroug overview of the framework while having one of the industry experts as my mentor.
